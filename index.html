Homemade food served in the heart of Wymondham

Welcome to the Lemon Tree

Located in the heart of Wymondham within easy walking distance of the abbey and the train station, The Lemon Tree serves freshly-prepared, homemade food that is made using local-sourced produce where ever possible. We are always happy to consider out customers needs and pride ourselves on our inclusive menu that always contains vegan and dairy-free options. So please pop in and enjoy a bite to eat in our unique and cosy café!
