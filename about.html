All About Us

Located in the heart of the historic market town of Wymondham, the Lemon Tree Café is a family business that opened its doors in August 2015 and has been serving satisfied customers ever since. Our café is located in a 14th Century building just a stones-throw from Wymondham Abbey and within easy walking distance of the train station.

Whilst we had no prior experience of Café culture, we adapted quickly and now have a thriving business known for its friendly atmosphere. Everyone is welcome and, if you pop in to see us, you’ll be greeted warmly in our cosy and comfortable corner of Wymondham.

It's a beautiful old building, too, which makes for a nice atmosphere.

At the Lemon Tree, we are known for providing great customer service and for our can-do approach (we will always try our best to meet customer needs) that means our customers come back to visit us again and again. All food is freshly prepared, with our homemade breads and cakes - which are served daily - particularly popular.

We are proud of our hard-working staff and our customer reviews and look forward to welcoming both regular and new customers to The Lemon Tree Café / Bar in the future. We hope to see you soon!

The reception from the staff when we arrive, as we are becoming somewhat regulars, is always warm and friendly and the setting is just delightful.
